Would You Pray For Me?

Would you pray for me,
If I said I was down for the count?
Would you hold me,
When I can't even hold myself?

Would you lift me up,
When my legs forget how to stand
Would you listen,
When the words won't stop spilling out?

Would you sit in silence with me,
When all I need is your presence?
Would you kneel beside me,
When I can't pray on my own?

Can you lend me your faith,
Because mine is worn and weak?
Read with me, talk to me,
Hug me—and just be here.

To be held…
That's all I need right now.

I need help.
I need rest.
I need strength.

But I'm too proud to ask.
I want to rest,
But I don't know how.
I need hope—
Just enough
To keep going.
