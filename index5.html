The Phone Goes Two Ways

The phone goes two ways, I know this to be true.
But sometimes, Lord, I forget to reach for you.
Caught in my world, wrapped in my strife,
Dealing with the crumbling edges of my life.

My heary is heavy, my mind lost in pain.
The whispers od doubt falling like rain.
Yet, when You cross my mind, Lord, I pause and see
That maybe You're waiting , quietly, for me.

I fear I might disturb You, interrput Your grace,
So I hesitate, unsure, hiding my face.
But You , oh God always near, always close,
Reach out with love when I need it the most.

Your voice is gentle, like a long-lost friend,
Calling me back, helping me mend.
So even when I falter, lost in my day,
The phone goes two ways-You remind me to pray.

Though I forget, though I fail to call,
You wait with open arms, catching
me when I fall.
So, Lord, when I'm wrapped in my troubles so deep,
Remind me that You're there, never asleep.

And when I feel like I can't find the way,
May I remember, Lord, to dial Your name and pray.
