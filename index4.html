Dear God


Dear God, I'm scared, I'm terrified, Some days I want to run and hide, But firm I stand in what You've said, I come back running, not misled.

When I sin, I fear Your dismay, Afraid You'd turn Your love away, But that's not true, You welcome me, With open arms, You set me free.

For doubts I have, You bring to ruin, And in Your grace, I'm found again, I want to grow, to walk in love, Need You more than the air above.

God, I need You, every day, In every step, in every way, With gratitude, my heart does sing, Thankful for everything You bring.
